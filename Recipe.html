Oat Banana Bliss Smoothie Recipe

Ingrdients

  • ½ cup oats

  • 1 ripe banana

  • 2 tablespoons raisins

  • 1 tablespoon honey (adjust to taste)

  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon powder

  • 1 cup fresh milk (or more for desired consistency)

  • Ice cubes (optional, for a chilled smoothie)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients:
    oats, banana, raisins, honey,cinnamon, and fresh milk.

  2. Blend:
    In a blender, combine the oats, banana, raisins , honey, cinnamon, fresh milk and ice cubes. Blend until smooth and creamy.

  3. Adjust Consistency:
    Add more milk if you prefer a thinner smoothie or a few ice cubes for a colder drink. Blend again if needed.

  4. Serve:
    Pour the smoothie into a glass and sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon or a few raisins on top for garnish.

  5. Enjoy:
    Serve immediately and savor the wholesome goodness!
